Coorg

A place to give you refreshness and peace





Itinerary
Day 1: Mangalore-Coorg-
The journey starts from Mangalore. From here, our IHPL representative will guide you to every proposed destination.
Head to Coorg and reach there by evening. Have a sumptuous dinner, followed by an overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 2: Coorg-
Post breakfast, embark on a city tour of Coorg. To begin with, visit the holy Omkareshwar temple at Talacauvery.
After having a delicious lunch, retire yourself in the lap of nature at the breathtaking Abbey’s falls and Raja’s seat. Later, dinner and stay at the hotel.
Day 3: Coorg-
Fresh in the morning visit the Golden temple, which is located near Kushalnagar in the region of Coorg.
One of the biggest Tibetan monasteries of India, the temple lets you embrace Tibetan culture and religion in a profound way. Check in to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.
Day 4: Coorg-Mangalore-
After breakfast, proceed to Mangalore. From here, take the respective flights back to your home town.
