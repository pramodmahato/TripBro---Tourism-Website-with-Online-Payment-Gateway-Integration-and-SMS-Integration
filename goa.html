Goa

For All Young Party Minds Out There




Goa Goa is sun, sand and memories. On this day, you will take a flight from your departure city, arrive in Goa and get a private transfer to your hotel. Once you have checked-in, spend the day at leisure. Later, return to your hotel for a comfortable stay. Suggested: You can head out to Anjuna Beach, famous for its white sand and red laterite rocks, for a relaxing afternoon. After which, you can stop over at Vagator Beach, and enjoy a picturesque sunset. For a night of partying, a visit to Arambol Beach, would be ideal. (Please note, that these are only suggested activities and are not included in the package.)


Itinerary
Day-1:
Reach hotel till 10 am. Do Lunch and rest till 1pm. Go to Baga Beach/Calangute Beach. Stay there till mid night. Baga Beach, Calangute Beach.
Day-2:
Morning: Fort Aguada and Fort Chhapora(DCH fame).
Afternoon: “Arambol beach” Full day includes the visit at sweet water lake there.
Evening: “Saturday Night Bazar” at Arpora (near Baga).
Night: (Night there starts at 10, pubs will remain open till late night) : Go to Tito’s bar (One of the best in Goa. If you don’t get entry there go to any other pub, all are good. Just see the quality of crowd before entering.)
Day-3:
Now it’s the time to see the real beauty of Goa… it's hard to wake up early (after all, you had a great fun in pub last night ;) )
Early Morning: Go to South Goa Visit Palolem beach.
For all Day : Mobor beach, Varca beach, Bogmalo beach, Agonda beach, BetalBatim Beach, Colva beach, benaulim beach. Choose any of them, all are damn beautiful.
Evening: Reserve this evening for the casino cruise ship at Panjim jetty. Prior hitting the casino same day second half you may plan tour to Dona Poula, Miramar, spend some time to see Malls in Goa.
Night: Don’t plan to sleep, it is your last night in goa. Go to Anjuna beach, stay there till 3-4 AM.
Day-4:
Go to calangute and do some Shopping from local market. You can do temporary or permanent tattoo on your body there. Don’t forget to bring some liquor for your friends ;)
Don’t be late on this day. We stuck there last time because of high wind and rain. Reach airport/station 2 Hours before time. You will have to join the office next day :P
